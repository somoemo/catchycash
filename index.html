Cac Loai Ty Le Keo Pho Bien Va Ung Dung Trong Thuc Te

  • Keo Chau A (Handicap): Giup can bang the tran giua hai doi qua do chon doi co kha nang thang keo cao hon.
  • Keo Chau Au (1X2): Don gian hon voi ba lua chon: thang, hoa, thua.
  • Keo Tai Xiu: Dua tren tong so ban thang trong tran dau, phu hop voi nhung ai thich dat cuoc theo kich ban tong the tran dau.
  • Keo dac biet: Bao gom cac ty le phu nhu chap phat goc, chan/le, hoac cac ty le cuoc theo tung giai dau dac thu.

Phan tich ty le keo dung cach se giup ban nhan biet nhung co hoi tot nhat, dong thoi tranh khoi cac bay cua nha cai.

  • Khi ty le thay doi theo chieu huong khong thuan loi: Nguoi choi nen canh giac hoac tam dung cuoc.
  • Phan tich nguyen nhan cua bien dong: Co the la do thong tin moi ve doi hinh, chan thuong, hoac cac yeu to tam ly khac.
